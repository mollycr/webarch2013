The ambassador for 145 years of graduates

Support. Honor. Invest. New. Excellence.

‹ ›

For many Port Huron area citizens, the sight and sounds of the Port Huron High School Marching Band evoke pride, joy and excitement. The band is an ambassador for 145 years of proud graduates, strong and capable athletes and the city of Port Huron.

As supporters of the band, we recognize that the band leaves an impression of our community.

We want them to shine, so that we all shine.

We want them to reflect a community with high standards, that values their young people.

Please take a moment to view these materials that explain the SHINE campaign, and consider making an investment in the Port Huron High School bands.
